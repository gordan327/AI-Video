# Vision

## Our Mission

AI-Video is an open and extensible privacy protection engine for video processing.

Our mission is simple:

> Protect people's privacy in videos without sacrificing usability.

We believe privacy protection should be accurate, reliable, and accessible to everyone.

---

# Why AI-Video?

As AI technology becomes more powerful, video analysis is becoming increasingly common.

However, privacy protection often remains an afterthought.

AI-Video was created to provide a reusable framework that enables developers to build privacy-aware video applications.

Rather than being a single-purpose tool, AI-Video aims to become a general privacy protection engine.

---

# Design Philosophy

AI-Video follows several core principles.

## Accuracy

Privacy protection should be reliable.

A single missed face may expose sensitive information.

AI-Video therefore combines:

- Face Detection
- Face Tracking
- Prediction
- Temporal Hold

to minimize privacy leakage.

---

## Extensibility

Every major component should be replaceable.

Current architecture already supports:

- Detector
- Tracker
- Renderer

Future algorithms can be integrated without redesigning the entire system.

---

## Simplicity

Developers should be able to understand the architecture quickly.

Components are designed with clear responsibilities and minimal coupling.

---

## Open Architecture

AI-Video is designed as an open framework.

Anyone should be able to develop new:

- Renderers
- Detectors
- Trackers

through a consistent plugin interface.

---

# Long-term Goals

Our long-term vision includes:

- Cross-platform desktop application
- Plugin ecosystem
- Third-party renderer support
- High-performance processing
- Open-source community collaboration

---

# Final Goal

We hope AI-Video becomes not only a useful application, but also a reusable framework for privacy protection in video processing.

Protect Privacy.
Preserve Freedom.
